About conference

Why R?

We are pleased to announce that WhyR 2018 will be organized by STWUR (Wroclaw R Users Group). The second official meeting of Polish R enthusiasts will be held in Wroclaw 2-4 July 2018. As the meeting is held in English, we are happy to invite R users from other countries.

This event is supported by European R user groups.

Conference Programme

Workshops

2nd of July: R workshops and tutorials. Additional mlr workshops are on the 4th of July.

Talks

3rd and 4th of July: plenary lectures (40 minutes including questions) and talks (20 minutes including questions) submitted by WhyR participants.

Hackathon

5th of July: collaborative work on the mlr package under the guidance of its developers.

Poster session

3rd of July:

Lightning Talks

4th of July: dynamic session of short talks. Presenters of lightning talks have 5 minutes at their disposal to present

Special Interest Groups Session

5th of July: meet R users with similar interests and share with them your experience. Topics are: Women in data science, Career growth with R and Teaching data analysis

Venue

Scandic Hotel Wrocław

Scandic Wrocław, ul. Piłsudskiego 49-57

With 10 versatile meeting and conference rooms, state-of-the-art meeting technology and individual conference menus, we can organise your event exactly as you want it to be. After your meeting or a bit of sightseeing, visit our restaurant and bar “My Corner” where you can try local specialities. You can also opt for a workout in our gym, followed by a relaxing moment in the sauna. At the end of the day, enjoy a pleasant night’s sleep in our rooms, done up in classic or modern Scandinavian design. Scandic Wroclaw is close to tons of popular sights, cosy cafés, theatres, the opera and philharmonic. We offer the perfect starting point for exploring the fascinating labyrinth of bridges and islands that make up this city. In just 10 minutes, you are at the market square in the historical old town or at the train station. Don’t miss out on the Africarium, a complex within Wroclaw Zoo that is unique in the world, dedicated to water-based African species and ecosystems. The airport is only a 30-minute drive away.
